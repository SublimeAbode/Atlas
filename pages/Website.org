# Short background and mission statement ended with a subscription form.

Insofar as all states of unhapiness are governed by causality within range of volition, Project Empyrean seeks maximally certain knowledge by which that volition may be constrained for the attainment of indefinite happiness. In other words, finding reliable ways to transcend the human condition into planes of heavenly existence and beyond.
